Our Process

As your Wedding Planner, we carefully craft experiences where we take care of everything, you'll want and need to create the wedding of your dreams. Our signature experience includes a 5 phase ____, but the details that fall in between will look completely different for each couple because those details will refelct you. At Grace Gutierrez Design we don't just make events look pretty, we create an experience for you and your guests that start and end with moments that you'll want to keep reliving. Our team focuses on the smallest of details that you very likely should never have to focus on.

BUDGET DEVELOPMENT

Our open communication starts at the very beginning with honest discussions about your financial investment and how it will influence your wedding.

LOCATION, VENDORS, & DESIGN

The look, the flow, the vibes. Choosing a venue and your vendor team is fundamental to our process and will decide the look and feel of your event. Just like building a house, you have to build your foundation before you select paint colors

Frequently Asked Questions

When in the planning process should we hire you?

The best time to start working with us is at the very beginning of your planning journey. Most couples usually start anywhere between 12 - 15 months before their wedding date.

Do you travel for Events?

Our team is based in New York and Cartagena, Colombia but we love to work with couples who live and want to celebrate anywhere.

Do you offer wedding day(day-of) coordination?

Absolutely, all of our packages include wedding day assistance.

Do you plan other events, such as rehearsal dinners?

Yes! All of our packages are custom tailored to what your needs are for your wedding weekend.
