My Approach

I know the feeling and warmth of truly being heard, also in dealing with family dynamics and the interplay that goes on in those situations. We make sure everyone is actually being listened to and understood. That human interaction and getting good vibes is what is gonna make you go from uncomfortable staring at a camera and not knowing where to place your hands to interacting with your loved ones in a honest and authenticate way to truly represents you. We'll always be there but never interrupting moments.

FAQ

LOOK AND FEEL BEAUTIFUL IN FRONT OF A CAMERA

First of all dont worry, we all get uncomfortable or anxious from time to time and especially come Wedding Day, I was super anxious for mines and in rushing almost forgot to finish writing my vows (ask me about this and I'll give you the full story). I've learned throughout the years how to help people through these moments to feel calm with whether its with a soothing voice of reassurance or maybe you just need a drink to calm those nerves down a bit. I've been in enough of these scenarios to notice and step in and direct you to help you find your best angles and anticpate what you need and be there for you.

FAQ

WORRIED ABOUT YOUR FAMILY DYNAMICS

So what does it look like to work with us

01
  • Discovery Call

After an initial inquiry, we'll hop on a video call or in person meetup to chat and get a vibe for one another. We'll see what matters to you, and find out and piece together what you have envisioned for your dream day.

02
  • Strategy Planning

This is the nitty gritty, where we work together to help you navigate your day and sculpt the timeline of events and photos that are your must haves for your wedding, and dive deep into curating something truly special and get that excitement going for your dream day.

03
  • Wedding Day

Woohoo! The fun begins and magic happens. Your day will be documented in its purest form on top of all the in between moments and captruing the little details that mean the most in telling the story of your entire day. For a wedding day, I end up delivering on average 60 - 100 photos per hour in a day.

04
  • Deliverables

Its time to sit back and bask on your photos, I always suggest you grab your favorite drink of choice and go through your photos together and reminisce on how special you felt that day, make it a date night while your at it. Your high resolution images will be delivered in 4 - 6 weeks. Your images will be hosted on a online private gallery with a password protected and shareable link and get to view your day from so many perspectives.
